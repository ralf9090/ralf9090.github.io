PawAI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

PawAI ("we", "our", "the app") is a pet care assistant application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

PawAI collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your name (for personalization)
  • Pet information (name, breed, age, weight, health records)
  • Medication schedules and feeding logs
  • Chat history with the AI Vet assistant
  • App preferences and settings

All pet data and chat history are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not upload, sync, or store your personal data on external servers.

AI Service

When you use the AI Vet feature, your questions and pet context data are sent to our secure server proxy to generate responses. This data is:

  • Processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers
  • Used solely to generate AI responses for your queries
  • Not shared with third parties for marketing or profiling purposes

Advertising

PawAI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • General device information for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - Advertising services
  • AI API Provider - AI response generation (via secure proxy)

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures. AI communications are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Children's Privacy

PawAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings > Delete All Data option. Since data is stored locally, deleting it removes it permanently.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
