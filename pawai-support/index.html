FAQ

Q: Is PawAI a real veterinarian?
A: No. PawAI uses AI to provide general pet care information only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical advice.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All your pet data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your data to external servers.

Q: How do I delete my data?
A: Go to Settings > Delete All Data to permanently remove all data from your device.

Q: Why do I need to watch an ad for AI insights?
A: PawAI is completely free. Ads help us keep the app free while covering AI processing costs.
