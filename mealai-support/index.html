Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a meal?

Tap the + button on the Home screen. Search for your food (e.g., "chicken rice"), select it from the list, adjust the portion size, and tap "Add Meal". If your food isn't in our database, tap "Enter manually" to add it with custom nutrition values.

How accurate are the nutrition values?

Our food database contains nutritional information for over 200 common foods based on standard reference values. Values are calculated per 100g and scaled to your portion size. For precise tracking, we recommend using a kitchen scale.

What are AI Insights?

AI Insights provides personalized nutrition analysis based on your daily meal log. Categories include Calorie Analysis, Macro Balance, Diet Suggestions, Weekly Summary, and Healthy Alternatives. The first insight each day is free; additional insights require watching a short ad.

Is AI Insights medical advice?

No. AI Insights are for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical or dietary advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for dietary decisions.

How do I change my calorie goal?

Go to Profile tab and tap "Daily Calorie Goal" to update your target.

How do I delete a meal?

On the Home screen, swipe left on any meal entry to delete it.

Is my data safe?

All your meal data is stored locally on your device. Only AI Insight queries are sent to our secure server (via HTTPS). We do not sell or share your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
