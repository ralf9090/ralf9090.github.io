Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Introduction

Glow Up AI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos

When you use Glow Up AI, you may take or select photos for face analysis. These photos are:

  • Sent to our AI service provider (Alibaba Cloud / DashScope) for analysis
  • NOT stored on our servers after analysis is complete
  • Stored locally on your device only (for history feature)
  • Never shared with third parties for marketing purposes

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data including:

  • Number of analyses performed
  • App crash reports
  • General usage statistics

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad personalization
  • Usage data for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered face analysis
  • To display your analysis history
  • To improve the app experience
  • To display relevant advertisements

Data Storage

Your analysis results and photos are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a server-side database of user photos or results.

Third-Party Services

  • Alibaba Cloud (DashScope) - AI face analysis processing
  • Google AdMob - Advertising

Children's Privacy

Glow Up AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app.
